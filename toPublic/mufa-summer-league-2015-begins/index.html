MUFA Summer League 2015 Begins

Posted by: Seth Lakowske

The MUFA summer league has begun and 8″ Floppy Disk took to the field last night for the first time in a year.   We were a little rusty, but managed to come together at the end to score a few more points.  The final score 8″ Floppy Disk: 6, Alpacas 4.0 A New Hope: 13.
