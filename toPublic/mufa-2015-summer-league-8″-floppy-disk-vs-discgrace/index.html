Mufa 2015 Summer League – 8″ Floppy Disk vs. DiscGrace

Posted by: Seth Lakowske

8″ got back to its winning ways against a very stout defense in DiscGrace. DiscGrace played a disciplined zone that made it tough for 8″ to advance the disc up the field, but with enough persistence 8″ managed to get just enough production to edge out DiscGrace.

8″ Floppy Disk 11; DiscGrace 10
