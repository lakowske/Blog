Mufa 2015 Summer League – 8″ Floppy Disk vs. Discertators

Posted by: Seth Lakowske

8″ Floppy Disk had their first win last night! Strong defense and the what might be this season’s hardest fought point demonstrated the teams grit and determination. The offense had enough movement and playmaking to keep 8″ out front throughout the game.

8″ Floppy Disk 12; Discertators 8
