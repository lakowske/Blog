Mufa 2015 Summer League – 8″ Floppy Disk vs. Deep Throw It

Posted by: Seth Lakowske

8″ came away with a tie last night after mounting a late game comeback. The women were shorthanded and Deep Throw It’s patient offense made it tough for 8″‘s defense. In the end, 8″ overcame a two point deficit with two beautiful throws and catches making it a tie when time expired.

8″ Floppy Disk 10; Deep Throw It 10
